About Hills of Steel

Hills of Steel is an exciting physics-based tank action game where you drive through rugged hills and battle against opponents. Use your skills to upgrade tanks, collect loot, and defeat powerful bosses in this addictive mobile game brought to browsers.

Features

  • Multiple tanks to unlock and upgrade
  • Realistic physics and fun gameplay
  • Intense PvP battles
  • Offline mode available
  • Boss fights and endless gameplay modes

How to Play

Use the on-screen controls to move your tank, aim your cannon, and fire. Master the terrain and gravity to outmaneuver enemies and climb steep hills. Upgrade your tank with coins you earn during battle.

Tips and Tricks

  • Always upgrade your tank's armor and weapons first
  • Use hills to your advantage for shooting angles
  • Watch enemy patterns before attacking
